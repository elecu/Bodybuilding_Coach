\section*{Scope}
This document compiles stage format, compulsory/mandatory poses, and category notes from the official federation documents provided (UKBFF, PCA, WNBF UK) plus the BNBF and NABBA criteria text supplied by the user. \textbf{No additional (invented) criteria have been added.} Where a document did not provide a fixed mandatory-pose list, the text is reproduced as-is (e.g., “competitor chooses poses”).

\paragraph{Age eligibility notes (as provided).}
\begin{itemize}
\item Juniors’ categories: 16–23 years of age. An athlete may compete as a junior until 31 December of the year they become 23 years old. For 2025, a junior has to be born between 2002 and 2009 (inclusive), as stated by UKBFF.
\item Masters’ categories: Female Over 35 years; Male Over 40 / 50 / 60 years. Females may compete as a master from 1 January of the year they become 35 (for 2025: born 1990 or before) and 40 (for 2024 statement: born 1985 or before, as stated by UKBFF). Males may compete as a master from 1 January of the year they become 40 (for 2025: born 1985 or before), 50 (born 1975 or before), and 60 (born 1965 or before), as stated by UKBFF.
\item UKBFF note: categories may be split/combined or additional height/weight classes added depending on entries, per UKBFF officials present at the event.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection*{Men’s Classic Bodybuilding (UKBFF)}
\textbf{Eligibility/Classes:} Classic-Bodybuilding categories are subject to a height/weight restriction (per UKBFF).\par
\textbf{Height/weight classes (verbatim from provided doc):}
\begin{itemize}
\item subject to the following criteria:
\item UP TO AND INCLUDING 168 CM CATEGORY: maximum body weight = body height [cm] minus 100 (Example: A competitor, who is 167 cm tall, may weigh a maximum of 67 kg)
\item UP TO AND INCLUDING 171 CM CATEGORY: maximum body weight = body height [cm] minus 100 + 2 kg (Example: A competitor, who is 170 cm tall, may weigh a maximum of 72 kg)
\item UP TO AND INCLUDING 175 CM CATEGORY: maximum body weight = body height [cm] minus 100 + 4 kg (Example: A competitor, who is 174 cm tall, may weigh not more than 78 kg)
\item UP TO AND INCLUDING 180 CM CATEGORY maximum body weight = body height [cm] minus 100 + 7 kg (Example: A competitor, who is 180 cm tall, may weigh not more than 87 kg)
\item OVER 180 CM CATEGORY (this category has 3 sub height/weight) 180.2 – 190 cm = body height [cm] minus 100 + 9 kg (i.e. height 189 cm max. weight 89 + 9 = 98 kg) 190.2 – 198 cm = minus 100 + 11 kg (i.e. height 197 cm max. weight 97 + 11 = 108 kg) Over 198 cm = minus 100 + 13 kg (i.e. height 204 cm max. weight 104 + 13 = 117 kg)
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Judging emphasis (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Classic look and presentation; emphasis on symmetry, flow, and aesthetics in addition to muscularity/conditioning (per UKBFF Classic Bodybuilding rules).
\item Note: “There will be no Most Muscular.” (per UKBFF Classic Bodybuilding rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Stage format / rounds (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Prejudging: relaxed quarter turns + compulsory poses.
\item Finals: free posing routine + compulsory poses.
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Compulsory / mandatory poses (explicit list):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Front Double Biceps
\item Front Lat Spread
\item Side Chest (any side)
\item Side Triceps (any side)
\item Back Double Biceps
\item Back Lat Spread
\item Abdominals and Thighs
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Routine / walk:} Free posing routine (music) (per UKBFF Classic Bodybuilding rules; timings per show/promoter).\par
\textbf{Attire:} Men must wear black posing trunks with no logos; thongs are not permitted (per UKBFF Classic Bodybuilding rules).\par

\begin{itemize}

\item - 2. Over 168cm Up to \& incl. 171 cm Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] – 100) plus SIX (6) Kgs
\item - 3. Over 171cm Up to \& incl. 175 cm Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] -100) plus EIGHT (8) Kgs
\item - 4.Over 175cm Up to \& incl. 180 cm Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] -100) plus ELEVEN (11) Kgs
\item - 5. Over 180 cm:
\item a) over 180 cm up to \& incl. 188 cm: Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] -100) plus THIRTEEN (13) Kgs
\item b) over 188 cm up to \& incl. 198 cm: Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] – 100) plus FIFTEEN (15) kgs
\item c) over 196 cm: Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] – 100) plus SEVENTEEN (17) kgs
\end{itemize}

\item - Up to \& incl. 175 cm Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] -100) plus FOUR (4) kilos
\item - Up to \& incl. 180 cm Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] -100) plus SIX (6) kilos

\item c) over 196 cm: Max Weight [kg] = (Height [cm] – 100) plus NINE (9) Kgs

\item Compulsory pose round includes the mandatory pose list below.

\subsubsection*{Men’s Physique (UKBFF)}
\textbf{Judging emphasis (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Symmetry, shape, proportion, stage presence (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules / rulebook).
\item Not a bodybuilding-style “most muscular” division; competitors present in a fixed but not flexed stance (per Men’s Physique rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Stage format / rounds (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Four quarter turns: Front → Quarter Turn Right → Back → Quarter Turn Right → Front (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Compulsory / mandatory poses (explicit list):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Front: standing relaxed with one hand on hip (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\item Quarter Turn Right: standing relaxed with one hand on hip (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\item Back: standing relaxed with one hand on hip (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\item Quarter Turn Right: standing relaxed with one hand on hip (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\item Front: standing relaxed with one hand on hip (per UKBFF Men’s Physique rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Routine / walk:} No compulsory posing routine specified in Men’s Physique rules; show format may include model walk depending on event (follow promoter instructions).\par
\textbf{Attire:} Physique shorts (per UKBFF rules; details vary by show).\par

\subsubsection*{Women’s Physique (UKBFF)}
\textbf{Judging emphasis (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Muscular development and symmetry with feminine athletic look (per Women’s Physique rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Stage format / rounds (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Four quarter turns (front/right/back/right/front) for assessment.
\item Compulsory poses are called in the stated order.
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Compulsory / mandatory poses (explicit list):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Front Double Biceps (both arms raised and flexed; feet shoulder width; abdominals and quads flexed).
\item Side Triceps (keep body turned sideways; show upper-body muscle; keep legs straight but flexed).
\item Back Double Biceps (both arms up; calves and hamstrings flexed; look straight).
\item Abdominals and Thighs (hands behind head; display abdominals; front leg extended and flexed).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Routine / walk:} Posing routine may be included per show; follow promoter instructions.\par
\textbf{Attire:} Women’s physique suit (per UKBFF).\par

\subsubsection*{Bikini (UKBFF)}
\textbf{Judging emphasis (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Overall stage presence, physique presentation, balance; division-specific emphasis on lines and presentation (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Stage format / rounds (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Comparison round: quarter turns (front, right, back, right, front).
\item Finals: Stage walk / I-walk (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Compulsory / mandatory poses (explicit list):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Front stance: bikini pose with weight on one leg, shoulders back, arms relaxed (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\item Quarter turn face right: right leg to side and slightly back; left hand on hip; right arm relaxed; head turned to face judges (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\item Quarter turn face back: both feet staggered; display glutes/hamstrings; arms relaxed; head turned to judges (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\item Quarter turn face right: mirror of other side (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\item Face front again: return to front stance (per UKBFF Bikini rules).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Routine / walk:} Finals: Stage walk sequence is directed by head judge (per UKBFF Bikini rules).\par
\textbf{Attire:} Bikini suit and heels (per UKBFF).\par

\subsubsection*{Men’s Bodybuilding (WNBF UK, 2025)}
\textbf{Judging emphasis (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Overall bodybuilding criteria: muscularity + conditioning + symmetry/presentation (per WNBF UK document).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Stage format / rounds (as stated):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Round 1 – Symmetry: quarter turns (Front, Right Side, Back, Left Side).
\item Round 2 – Muscularity: mandatory poses (listed below).
\item Round 3 – Free posing: 60-second routine to competitor’s music (per WNBF UK document).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Compulsory / mandatory poses (explicit list):}
\begin{itemize}
\item Front Double Biceps pose (heels together; elbows slightly down; biceps peaked; calves, hamstrings, glutes, quads flexed).
\item Front Lat Spread pose (heels together; lats flared; biceps not flexed; thighs/calf muscles flexed).
\item Side Chest pose (either side).
\item Side Triceps pose (either side).
\item Rear Double Biceps pose (heels together; elbows slightly down; biceps peaked; calves, hamstrings, glutes flexed).
\item Rear Lat Spread pose (heels together; lats flared; biceps not flexed; calves, hamstrings, glutes flexed).
\item Abs and Thigh pose (both arms overhead, flexing down on the abdominals).
\item Most Muscular pose (any of the three styles; hands can’t be clasped).
\end{itemize}
\textbf{Routine / walk:} Free posing routine: 60 seconds only; props/costumes permitted but not required (per WNBF UK document).\par
\textbf{Attire:} Men’s posing trunks (per WNBF UK).\par
